Our History

Founded in 2000, Vehicles Corporation has grown from a small, local storage facility to a renowned provider of comprehensive vehicle storage services. Over the years, we have expanded our facilities, improved our security measures, and enhanced our customer service to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

  • 2000: Company founded
  • 2005: Expanded to three new locations
  • 2010: Introduced climate-controlled storage
  • 2015: Launched online booking system
  • 2020: Achieved ISO certification for quality management
